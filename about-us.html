About Intentium

Simply Put…We produce exceptional leads for our clients while simultaneously giving back to great causes. That is why for every campaign we run; we donate to local dog shelters, humane societies, and nonprofit. We want to make your marketing work for good; while providing the very best customer support, sales follow up assistance and campaigns to our clients.

The Demand Dogs team has been in sales and marketing positions all around the world over the past 25 years. Our goal is to help merge the gap between sales and marketing.

We understood early on how easy it can be to forget to give back, especially when we are busy. We asked ourselves: “How can we ‘do good,’ while producing a profit?;” this is how Demand Dogs was born.

As dog lovers and demand generation specialists, we found a way to merge our passions in a way that serves our clients and all while sticking to our core values.
